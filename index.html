About Us

Shree Ram Crop Science, is a leading Indian company having a strong foothold in the Indian agrochemical industry. Setting up benchmarks in the formulation and marketing of pesticides and other agrochemicals, with a strong commitment to quality and a mission to make available the most effective, economical and environmentally sustainable pesticides and agrochemicals to the farmers.

We’re here to help you cultivate a safer, healthier environment. Whether you have questions about our products, need expert advice, or want to explore solutions tailored to your specific needs, our team is ready to assist you.

SRCS is an ISO 9001-2008 as well as ISO 14001-2004 accredited company and is run by highly qualified professional in all the relevant fields. The continous support and immense faith of farmers in SRCS helps us to provide new molecules each year for betterment of farmers with their immediate acceptance.
